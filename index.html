    • Pickleball

    Pickleball is an engaging and fast-growing sport that combines elements of tennis, badminton, and ping-pong. Played on a smaller court with a net, players use a solid paddle to hit a lightweight, perforated ball. The game can be played in singles or doubles, with the objective being to score points by successfully hitting the ball over the net and into the opponent’s court. Pickleball is popular for its accessibility and ease of learning, making it suitable for all ages and skill levels. Its blend of strategy, agility, and fun has contributed to its widespread appeal across various communities.

    Hiking/Camping

    Hiking the Appalachian Trail is a challenging and rewarding adventure stretching over 2,190 miles from Georgia to Maine. Trekkers navigate diverse landscapes, from lush forests to rugged mountains, experiencing breathtaking views and diverse wildlife. The journey offers a profound connection with nature and a sense of accomplishment upon completion.

    Disc Golf

    Disc golf is a fun, outdoor sport where players throw a flying disc towards a series of metal baskets, aiming to complete each hole in as few throws as possible.
